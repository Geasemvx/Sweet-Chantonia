Nyathi Tonia Chantel

Hello, Welcome to Sweet Chantonia’s Business

Nyathi Tonia Chantel was born and raised in South Africa Phalaborwa-Mashishimale in Limpopo province and currently living in Namakgale . Matriculated in 2022 and furthered her skills with a professional baking training at Mopani College.A founder and head baker of Sweet Chantonia that brings delightful range of baked goods such as scones and biscuits.

My Story

We started Sweet Chantonia in 2024 with one simple goal: to bring homemade, fresh bakery treats to our community. What began as a small passion quickly grew into a place where flavors and memories meet.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to deliver high-quality baked goods while making every customer feel valued and welcomed. We believe in freshness, honesty, and we do our baking with love.

Why Is My Product Special?

What makes us different is our commitment to using only fresh ingredients and baking everything by hand every morning. No shortcuts, just real baking and prayer.

Friendly Call to Action

We’d love to bake something special for you. Visit our shop or place an order today and experience the Sweet Chantonia difference.
